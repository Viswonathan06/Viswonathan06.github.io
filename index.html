About

Viswonathan is currently a M.S. Computer Science student at UNC Chapel Hill focusing on research at the intersection of AI and Sustainability at the SAIL Lab at the School of Data Science and Society.

His research interests are in multi-modal machine learning, human-computer interaction, fairness and bias and computer vision.

Previously a Software Developer at Wells Fargo focusing on backend engineering, designing micro-frontends and data pipelines in the Personal Lending business.
